Grants

If you are experiencing reduced mobility,

local authority grants are available to assist with modifications to your home.

Get in touch

We specialise in Grant Work and offer our customers full assistance to all aspects of Grant Application including a step-by-step process on all matters of paperwork. We work in conjunction with a wide range of Local Authorities including Dublin City Council, South Dublin County Council, Fingal County Council, Dun Laoghaire â€“ Rathdown County Council, Kildare County Council, Wicklow County Council, Meath County Council and Louth County Council amongst others.

Two grant schemes are available

  • Mobility Aids Grant Scheme
  • Housing Adaptation Grant for people with a disability, this is for more extensive work within the home

Grant approveal must be obtained before the work to your home can be started. The contractor must also submit their Tax Clearance Certificate (C2) to the local authority.
