Occupational Therapists (OTs) help people of all ages to improve their ability to perform tasks in their daily living environments.

The role of an Occupational Therapist is to work with a client to help them achieve a fulfilled and satisfied state in life.

Occupational Therapists work in many different practice settings including hospitals, day care facilities, nursing homes, schools, universities, community centres and workplaces. Many also work in private practice and provide occupational therapy in the clientâ€™s home or residential setting.

Why do I need an Occupational Therapist?

Occupational Therapist will recommend solutions that best suit your requirements if you;

  • a. are applying for a Home Adaptation Grant with your County Council.
  • b. are considering adapting your home.
  • c. have a condition which is continuing to affect your mobility.
